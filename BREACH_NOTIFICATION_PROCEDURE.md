# Breach Notification Procedure

---

## Internal (Plain-English)

### Notification Clock
- Starts at discovery, not confirmation
- Log timestamp of first detection

### 72-Hour Rule
- Notify affected restaurant owners and users within 72 hours if health or consumer risk is possible

### Notification Templates
#### Restaurant Owner
- What happened
- What data was involved (general categories)
- What we are doing
- What you should do (e.g., reset passwords)
- Contact protocol

#### End User
- What happened
- What data was involved (general categories)
- What we are doing
- What you should do
- Contact protocol

#### Regulatory (if food safety implicated)
- What happened
- What data was involved (general categories)
- What we are doing
- What you should do
- Contact protocol

### Secure Messaging
- Use secure channel (no email with details)
- Provide non-technical summary for brand protection

---

## External (Legal/Regulatory)

### Notification Requirements
- Notification clock starts at discovery
- 72-hour reporting guideline for health/consumer risk
- Notify regulatory authorities if food safety implicated

### Template Structure
- Incident summary
- Data categories involved
- Remediation steps
- User/owner instructions
- Contact protocol

### Fail Safes
- "No breach" confirmation template
- Secure messaging channel
- Non-technical summary for public/brand protection
