# MealScout Founder-Led Pitch (Food Trucks)

## 10-second opener (in person / phone)

“I’m building **MealScout** for food trucks. We have **guaranteed parking locations** you can book like a calendar. **$25/month** for premium, and when you book a spot we add a **$10 transaction fee**. Where are you parked this week? I can get you set up in 10 minutes.”

## 30-second version (use when they engage)

“MealScout is the booking layer between **trucks** and **hosts** (breweries, bars, offices, event spaces). Hosts list paid slots; you book instantly. You get a public profile, a schedule, and premium tools like **live GPS** and a booking calendar so customers can find you. If you tell me your usual hours/areas, I’ll match you with a few locations and you can book your first spot today.”

## “Irresistible offer” framing (what to say)

- “I’m not asking you to try a beta—**payments and booking are live**.”
- “You’ll spend less time DM’ing venues and more time selling food.”
- “If you’ve ever been stuck hunting for a spot last-minute, this fixes that.”

## Objection killers (short, non-pushy)

- **“We already have spots”** → “Perfect—this just adds backup options and better nights. What’s your slowest day this week?”
- **“I don’t want another subscription”** → “Totally fair. If it doesn’t book you 1 good night, cancel. The point is *more predictable locations*.”
- **“How do I know hosts are real?”** → “Hosts are verified and paid listings; booking is confirmed only after payment.”

## What to highlight from Premium ($25/mo)

- **Public profile** that acts like a website (menu/photos/social/contact)
- **Live GPS button** for instant map presence (premium)
- **Editable schedule + booking calendar view** (premium)
- **Discover + book** parking pass spots (core)
- **Notifications** for booking updates and schedule changes

## DM template (Instagram)

“Hey {truck_name} — I’m local and building MealScout. We help trucks **book guaranteed parking spots** instantly (no back-and-forth DMs). Premium is **$25/mo** and booking adds a **$10 fee**. If I send you 2–3 spots that match your hours this week, would you try booking one?”

## Follow-up cadence

- Day 0: DM + 1 question (“Where are you parked this week?”)
- Day 2: Send 2–3 matched spots + ask for 10-min onboarding call
- Day 5: Quick check-in + “Want me to set your profile/schedule for you?”
