# Pensacola Food Truck Location Playbook (Daily Tracking)

Goal: figure out **where a truck is today** (and get a direct contact) in <5 minutes per lead.

## Fastest ways (in order)

1. **Instagram Stories** (best signal)
   - Check Stories + Highlights for: “schedule”, “today”, “where we are”, “weekly”, “events”.
   - Tap their **tagged location** (often the host venue name/address is there).
   - Check “Tagged” posts for recent venue tags (brewery, bar, market, office park).

2. **Facebook Page**
   - Many trucks post day-of locations as text posts.
   - Look for pinned posts with weekly schedules.

3. **Google**
   - Search: `"<truck name>" Pensacola food truck` + `schedule` / `today` / `location`.
   - Check Google Business profile (some use “Updates” posts).

4. **Host venues (reverse lookup)**
   - Identify recurring venues (breweries, bars, markets, event nights).
   - Check the venue’s social/calendar: they often announce “food truck tonight”.

5. **Events calendars**
   - Downtown events, farmers markets, brewery calendars, “food truck rally” posts.

## Data to capture every time (paste into your CRM / hit list)

- Today’s venue name + full address (or Google Maps link)
- Service window (start/end time)
- Primary contact channel (IG DM / phone / email)
- Screenshot or link to the post/story (proof)

## Quick scripts you can use while tracking

- DM opener: “Saw you’re at **{venue}** today. I’m local—can I send you 2–3 guaranteed parking spots that match your hours this week?”
- Phone opener: “Where are you parked this week? I’m building MealScout—instant booking for guaranteed spots. I can onboard you in 10 minutes.”
